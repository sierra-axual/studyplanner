How to Use Study Planner

  1. Add Your Modules: Start by adding your modules, including the number of assignments, due dates, and study hours required.
  2. Set Your Study Schedule: Configure which days of the week you can study and how many hours are available on each day.
  3. Customize Holidays and Leave Days: Select which holidays you can use for studying and add any leave days you plan to take.
  4. View Your Study Plan: Go to the Guide tab to see your personalized study plan in a calendar format.
  5. Export Your Plan: Download your study plan in PDF, HTML, or Excel format for offline reference.
